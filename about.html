What is mindfulness?

Mindfulness means maintaining a moment by moment awareness of our thoughts, feelings, bodily sensations and surrounding enviroment.

How does Mindfulness help mental health?

Mindfulness helps us pay more attention to the present moment. It help become more aware of our thoughts and feels so that, instead of being overwhelmed, we can manage them better.

What is Yoga?

Yoga is a group of physical, mental and spiritual practices that aim to control and still the mind.

How does Yoga help mental health?

Yoga has been shown to lower stress hormones and increase the release endorphins and GABA(gamma-aminobutyric acid). these feel good chemicals help decrease anxiety and improve your mood.
